EYE Lateral

EYE Lateral is targeting EYE Reasoning using Trealla Prolog.

Run the examples and test cases via 

$ ./test
and the results should be like in test results for EYE Lateral.

The transformation from and to N3 should be obvious with wrapper/2.

Background

  • Invited paper of John Alan Robinson: Computational Logic: Memories of the Past and Challenges for the Future
  • Book of Markus Triska: The Power of Prolog
